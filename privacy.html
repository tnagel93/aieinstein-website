Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 12, 2025

Introduction

AiEinstein ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AiE mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photos:

  • Our app requires camera access to capture photos of your medication list
  • Photos are sent to our Cloud Functions (which use OpenAI's API) for plan generation and medication extraction
  • We do not store, save, or retain any photos you take through our app
  • Photos are only transmitted temporarily for analysis and are not stored by us

Account and Synced Data (when you sign in):

  • When you sign in with Apple, Google, or Email, we store your medication list, fitness and nutrition plans, and calendar assignments in Firebase Firestore
  • This allows your data to sync across your devices
  • We do not store medication images or raw photo data

Device Information:

  • We may collect basic device information necessary for app functionality
  • This includes device type, operating system version, and app version

How We Use Your Information

Plan Generation and Medication Analysis:

  • Photos you capture are sent to our Cloud Functions (using OpenAI's GPT-4o) for medication extraction and plan generation
  • The analysis produces fitness plans, nutrition plans, and parsed medications
  • We do not store photos; when you sign in, only your saved medication list, plans, and calendar data are synced to Firestore

App Functionality:

  • Device information is used to ensure proper app operation and compatibility
  • Camera access is used only to capture photos for plan generation

Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google):

  • We use Firebase for authentication (Sign in with Apple, Google, Email) and Firestore for syncing your medication list, plans, and calendar
  • Google's privacy policy applies to Firebase services

OpenAI API:

  • We use OpenAI's GPT-4o API (via Cloud Functions) to analyze medication photos and generate plans
  • Photos are sent to our Cloud Functions for processing; OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data sent to their service
  • We do not control how OpenAI handles your data once transmitted

Data Storage and Retention

  • Photos: We do not store any photos you take through our app
  • Medication list, plans, calendar: When you sign in, these are stored in Firebase Firestore and synced across your devices. You control your medication list.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be stored for app functionality

Data Security

  • Photos are transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure

Your Rights and Choices

Camera Access:

  • You can grant or deny camera access through your device settings
  • The app requires camera access to function

Data Deletion:

  • We do not store your photos. When signed in, your medication list, plans, and calendar are in Firestore. Contact us to request deletion of your account data.
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove it from your device

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app or on our website.

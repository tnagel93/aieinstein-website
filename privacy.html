Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 12, 2025

Introduction

AiEinstein ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our medication analysis application.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photos:

  • Our app requires camera access to capture photos of your medications
  • Photos are processed locally on your device and sent to OpenAI's API for analysis
  • We do not store, save, or retain any photos you take through our app
  • Photos are only transmitted temporarily to OpenAI for analysis and are not stored by us

Device Information:

  • We may collect basic device information necessary for app functionality
  • This includes device type, operating system version, and app version

How We Use Your Information

Medication Analysis:

  • Photos you capture are sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o model for medication identification
  • The analysis is performed by OpenAI's artificial intelligence service
  • We do not store or retain any of your photos or analysis results

App Functionality:

  • Device information is used to ensure proper app operation and compatibility

Third-Party Services

OpenAI API:

  • We use OpenAI's GPT-4o API to analyze medication photos
  • Photos are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing
  • OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data sent to their service
  • We do not control how OpenAI handles your data once transmitted

Data Storage and Retention

  • Photos: We do not store any photos you take through our app
  • Analysis Results: Analysis results are displayed on your device but not stored by us
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be stored for app functionality

Data Security

  • Photos are transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure

Your Rights and Choices

Camera Access:

  • You can grant or deny camera access through your device settings
  • The app requires camera access to function

Data Deletion:

  • Since we do not store your photos or analysis results, no data deletion is necessary
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove it from your device

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Bridging the Gap Between Hospital and Home

Understanding the challenge and how AiEinstein provides the solution

The Problem: Post-Discharge Confusion

Every year, millions of patients are discharged from hospitals with little understanding of what happened during their stay. They leave with a medication list, discharge papers filled with medical jargon, and countless unanswered questions:

  • What conditions do I actually have, and what do they mean for my daily life?
  • Why am I taking these medications, and what are the side effects I should watch for?
  • What foods are safe to eat with my conditions and medications?
  • What physical activities can I safely do during recovery?

This information gap leads to medication errors, dietary mistakes, unsafe physical activity, and preventable hospital readmissions. Patients deserve better.

The Solution: Intelligent, Personalized Guidance

AiEinstein transforms your medication list into a comprehensive, personalized recovery plan. Here's how our AI-powered app works:

📷

Photo Your Medication List

Take a photo of your medication list from your discharge papers. Our AI extracts each medication for you to review and confirm. Add them to My Medications with one tap, and they're automatically scheduled on your calendar.

📋

My Medications & Calendar

Log medications manually or from the photo. Set reminder times (daily, twice daily, at bedtime, etc.), track refills, and view your schedule by day. Sign in with Apple, Google, or Email to sync across devices.

💪

Fitness & Nutrition Plans

From your medication list, we generate personalized fitness and nutrition plans. Refresh plans anytime from My Medications. Plans include goals, weekly schedules, foods to emphasize or avoid, restrictions, and tips—all grounded in evidence-based guidelines.

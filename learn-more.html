Bridging the Gap Between Hospital and Home

Understanding the challenge and how AiEinstein provides the solution

The Problem: Post-Discharge Confusion

Every year, millions of patients are discharged from hospitals with little understanding of what happened during their stay. They leave with a medication list, discharge papers filled with medical jargon, and countless unanswered questions:

  • What conditions do I actually have, and what do they mean for my daily life?
  • Why am I taking these medications, and what are the side effects I should watch for?
  • What foods are safe to eat with my conditions and medications?
  • What physical activities can I safely do during recovery?

This information gap leads to medication errors, dietary mistakes, unsafe physical activity, and preventable hospital readmissions. Patients deserve better.
